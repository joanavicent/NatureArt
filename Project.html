Nature Art for kids

Art crafts with nature elements
that will encourage kids to explore mother nature



Life in urban centers has taken away childhood freedom, experiences and learnings that are essential to us.

As technology evolves, children seem to have more and more reasons to stay at home. However, we must create a balance and try to invest our time in a childhood with more contact with nature, in order to provide children more outdoor play and sensorial experiences.

With free materials that nature can provide us, it is possible to create authentic pieces of art, and simultaneously help children to evolve their respect for nature, their imagination, among many other aspects.

Check below some suggestions of easy crafts you can do with your kid!



With some branches, leaves, woolen and paint remnants, it is possible to create a beautiful frame.
It just requires some imagination and has a fantastic result: an unique piece of art whose artist is your child!



Tell your kids to choose some colors they like and then to paint directly on the leaves.
Transfer the painting to a white sheet: they have now a brand new picture, that was painted and printed by them!

Enjoy nature, create and have fun!
